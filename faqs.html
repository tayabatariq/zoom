Frequently Asked Questions

Before starting driving lessons, you must obtain a provisional driving licence. You can apply online through the official government website or by completing a D1 application form available at most Post Offices. Ensure you meet the minimum age requirement and provide necessary identification documents.

No, you can start practical driving lessons without having passed your theory test. However, passing the theory test is required before booking the practical driving test. Studying for the theory test alongside practical lessons can enhance your understanding and application of driving principles.

The number of lessons required varies for each individual, depending on factors such as prior experience, learning pace, and frequency of lessons. According to the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA), it takes an average of 44 hours of professional instruction plus 20 hours of private practice to pass the driving test. Our instructors will assess your progress and provide guidance on when you are ready to take the test.​

Please bring your provisional driving licence to each lesson. If you require corrective eyewear, ensure you wear it during lessons. Dress comfortably and choose appropriate footwear with thin soles to facilitate proper control of the vehicle's pedals.

The 6-Hour Intensive Course is designed for learners seeking focused instruction over a short period. The course includes six hours of one-on-one driving lessons with a certified instructor. If you opt for the fast-track test booking service, we will schedule your practical driving test within 2-6 weeks at a local test center. This comprehensive approach aims to prepare you thoroughly for the driving test in an expedited timeframe.

The 10-Hour Intensive Course offers ten hours of personalized driving instruction, allowing for more extensive coverage of driving skills and techniques. Similar to the 6-hour course, if you choose the fast-track test booking option, we will arrange your practical driving test within 2-6 weeks. This course is ideal for learners who prefer a more in-depth preparation before attempting the driving test.

If you prefer to arrange the test yourself, you can opt for the course without the fast-track test booking service. This option allows you to schedule the test at your convenience while still benefiting from our intensive instruction.

Our fast-track test booking service is designed to secure an earlier test date than typically available through standard booking channels. Upon enrolling in one of our intensive courses with the fast-track option, we will handle the scheduling of your practical driving test within 2-6 weeks at a local test center. This service aims to expedite the process of obtaining your driving licence.
